# Clear Cash Pitch Notes

## One-Line Pitch

Clear Cash tells freelancers how much money is actually safe to spend after upcoming bills, unpaid invoices, savings reserve, and emergency buffer.

## User

A Pakistani freelance developer, designer, editor, or writer who earns irregularly from direct clients, Fiverr, Upwork, or local agencies.

## Moment

They receive a payment or send an invoice and need to decide whether they can spend money this week without falling short before the next client payment arrives.

## Product Demo

1. Register or log in.
2. Open Dashboard.
3. Click `Load Demo Data`.
4. Show `Safe to Spend Now`.
5. Show the 30-day forecast and emergency buffer.
6. Open Invoices.
7. Pay or mark an invoice as paid.
8. Return to Dashboard and show the safer cash position.

## What Makes It FinTech

Clear Cash improves a real money decision: short-term cash planning for irregular income. It does not recommend investments, promise returns, or require bank access.

## Data Handling Answer

Clear Cash stores only email, hashed password, profile details, transactions, and invoices. It does not collect CNICs, card numbers, bank credentials, or investment data. User data is scoped by authenticated user ID.

## Limitations

Clear Cash is not a bank, accountant, lender, or financial advisor. It provides planning estimates only.
